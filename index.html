Travel Note

Transportation from Narita Airport to Hilton Tokyo:

Airport Limousine Bus (Orange-ish color ticket counter to purchase ticket (you can use AMEX card and the staff speaks English) will take you directly to Hilton Tokyo lobby (about 1.5 hours ): 3,100 Yen (about $27 (US) - varies based on the exchange rate fluctuation). Ticket counter staff will tell you where you can get on the bus (outside from the ticket counter and the bus station numbers are marked very clearly. Hilton Tokyo is the last stop so you can work or sleep in the bus. There is also a toilet in the rear of the bus. Important: Insure to tell the staff at the ticket counter that the destination is Hilton Tokyo located in Shinjuku. Please insure to keep your receipt for your TEAM processing (it is required).
